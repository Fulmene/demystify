# Mossbridge Troll
@MossbridgeTroll
@subset
@any number of untapped creatures you control other than SELF
 with total power 10 or greater
@(SUBSET (NUMBER any) (PROPERTIES untapped creatures
                       (control (PLAYER_SET you)))
  (NOT SELF)
  (TOTAL (POWER (NUMBER (GEQ 10)))))
